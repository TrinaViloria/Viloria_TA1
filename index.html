Travel

Going to different places has always been a passion of mine. I love gaining new experiences, trying their famous cuisines, and capturing the beauty of each destination through my lens.


DESTINO BEACH, BATANGAS

One of my most memorable trips was to Batangas, where I enjoyed the amazing view of the ocean while appreciating the beauty of nature surrounding it.


SKYRANCH, TAGAYTAY

This place is known for its cool climate and breathtaking views of Taal Volcano. I enjoyed the different rides in here, where the beauty of the surroundings contributed a lot to my enjoyment.


NORTHERN BLOSSOM FLOWER FARM, BENGUET

My trip to Benguet was an unforgettable experience. The beauty of the flowers and the breathtaking view made it a truly special place to visit.


BINONDO, MANILA

Exploring Binondo, the world's oldest Chinatown, was a great experience. The vibrant streets, rich history, and great food made it a must-visit destination for any traveler.


PERLAS NG SILANG, CAVITE

Visiting Cavite offered a peaceful and scenic experience. Its natural beauty made it a truly memorable trip.


INTRAMUROS, MANILA

Intramuros, provided a great view into the Philippines' history. Visiting this place felt like stepping back in time.


JONES BRIDGE, MANILA

The Jones Bridge, an iconic landmark in Manila, captivated me with its elegant design and historical significance. It was a pleasure to explore this beautiful structure and learn about its past.


GRAPE PICKING, LA UNION

It was my first time harvesting grapes, it was a great experience knowing how my favorite fruit are harvested.
